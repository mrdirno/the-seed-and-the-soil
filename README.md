**A Novel of Independence and Identity**

**Prism:** African Independence × Philosophical Dialogue × Individual vs Collective

## About

A 69,000-word philosophical novel exploring African independence through the lens of individual versus collective identity.

It honors the writers, thinkers, and dreamers whose work shaped these literary traditions.

**Part of the [persona500](https://persona500.com) research project.**

## The Prism

Why this combination?

- **African Independence** — Voices of liberation, finding power despite systems designed to exclude
- **Philosophical Dialogue** — The Socratic method applied to postcolonial identity
- **Individual vs Collective** — The tension every society navigates: personal vision versus mutual benefit
